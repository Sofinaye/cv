About Dwight

Dwight Kurt Schrute III (/ˈʃruːt/) is a character on the American television series The Office, portrayed by actor Rainn Wilson. Dwight is a salesman and assistant to Michael Scott at the fictional paper distribution company Dunder Mifflin, before his promotions in later seasons. He also runs a bed and breakfast at Schrute Farms, is a beet plantation owner, and in Season 7, becomes the owner of the business park in which Dunder Mifflin is located.
